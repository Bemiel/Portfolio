Vibe For Life

Vibe for life is a project I worked on for group projects. This is a course where we are tasked to make a project for a client within 10 weeks of production. We started of with 2 weeks of prototyping, followed by 5 weeks of production and then finally 2 weeks of game polish.



For this project, I took the role of game designer, sound and music developer, and general butterfly. This ranged from implementing and creating UI assets to assisting with code and art assets. The audio-oriented role allowed me to learn a new part of the production pipeline within a real project.

The project aimed to create an advertising game for Win For Life, designed as a casual, fun mobile game to promote Win For Life at smaller festivals. We decided on an endless runner featuring the parrot mascot from Win For Life as the protagonist. As the parrot, players fly and run through endless festival grounds, dodging obstacles and collecting music coins to keep their party flow going.



Main Contributions

Music

Creation of all music tracks, as well as implementing them into the game, and writing systems to transition between them throughout the game. More info about this is on my music page.

Sound

Creation & Implementation of all sound effects. Making of systems to more easily implement sound into the game. (Audiomanager, Sound "slots" for each obstacle collision...)

UI

Creation of all UI assets, and implementation of most UI.

All Around

Implementation of some obstacles and mechanics. Creating "chunks" (obstacle combinations) that appear throughout the game.
