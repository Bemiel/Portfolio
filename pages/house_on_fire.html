House On Fire

"House On Fire" was my groups' project for the Unwrap Game Jam. We teamed up with a few coders, and 2 (including myself) all-arounders. For this project I took the lead artist role, creating all art assets for the game, aswell as doing some level/game design.



The jam jam only lasted 2 days. My real challenge was to design, and work in an artstyle that allowed me to create assets really quickly.
